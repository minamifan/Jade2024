Poster Design

The graphic designs here are cultural, event, and promotional.
-The first cultural category mainly highlights the flavor of the Chinese Spring Festival, using illustrations to give people a warm feeling.
-The activity poster invites different families to participate in a small market activity. It is a parent-child activity, so the color selection and character personality are both relaxed and lively.
-The third promotional poster is mainly aimed at offline activities and calls on customers to participate in offline shopping.I chose funny and appealing characters to put in the middle

LOGO Design

-The first logo was made for an airline. Because it is related to aviation, I used a more abstract image of an airplane, inspired by paper airplanes. The color was blue in the sky, which is related to aerospace.
-The second company is a technology company. The company proposed to add the element of eyes, which means opening your eyes to a new world, so I made abstract eyes and added luminous special effects to enhance the sense of technology.
-Chapter 3 is still an airline company. The company requires Chinese and English names, so I used wings to separate the Chinese and English names, and the English words were matched with a metal word effect and integrated with the wings.
