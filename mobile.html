An app that helps students in shared housing find their favorite roommates in advance

For young adults who have just come to Canada or have had a bad experience of sharing a house, roommates play a very important role in the experience of sharing a house, so I want to develop and design a software that can understand the living habits and hobbies of roommates in advance, and even communicate with them. Roommates contacted the host to rent an apartment together.

Contribution

Whole precess:UX design, Graphic Design, Web Design, Proposal......

Team

Jade Fan

Duration

2 months

Idea enlightenment

In the last semester, the project of our group's ux research was to conduct a survey on unaffortable housing. In the real data of our survey, I found that most of the young adults rented houses, and most of the renters rented houses.
However, whether you get along well with roommates in a shared apartment is an important factor that affects the happiness index of shared apartments, so I came up with the idea of making such an app to match roommates in advance.

user research

After I conducted a simple questionnaire survey among friends around me.
I found that in the process of sharing a house, there are several important factors that affect the user experience: The first is location, second is price, and the third is roommates,if it friendly? The fourth is whether the house is new or old.

Persona

Is there a platform that allows users to learn about their roommate in advance?

Page logic diagram

In this app, there are two main lines. One is the same as the ordinary rental app, which has a house search function. In addition, there is another line which is the roommate search function. The premise of roommate search is that everyone has registered in the app. You always need to fill in your personal information, such as gender, living habits, etc.

Named-rentmate

I wanted to combine the naming of roommate and rent, so I finally positioned the name as rentmate, because both rent and roommate both start with R, so my logo is defined as two R's.

How to protect user privacy

During the APP design process, I thought about how to retain the personal information of roommates while protecting the privacy of users. I made the following settings:
1. The understanding of roommates is limited to gender, work and rest time, and hobbies.
2. There is no function to evaluate roommates. I know that airbnb has a review host function, so I was thinking about whether to add the review function to the host and roommate. In the end, I decided not to add it, so as not to cause pressure and psychological burden to users.
